## Methodology
**Ensemble causal inference model** combining three techniques (from incremental.com/how-it-works, verified April 2026):
1. Econometric techniques — historical statistical modeling
2. Designed experiments — planned holdout/test-control tests
3. Synthetic experiments — modeled counterfactuals for non-experiment periods

**Always-on learning loop:** daily prediction vs. actual delta injected back as recency signal.

**IAB/MRC compliant. PII-free.**

**Published accuracy metrics:** NONE — no public MAPE, R², or confidence interval benchmarks published as of April 2026. The Bayer case study reports 32% iROI improvement (business outcome, not measurement accuracy).

## Critical Finding: Shelf Signals as Control Variables — Not Outputs
**The definitive differentiation vs. DSIM.**

Incremental explicitly ingests: search rank, buy box ownership, inventory, competitive pricing, ratings & reviews.

**But these are ingested as control variables to neutralize confounders** — removing non-media factors to isolate media lift. They are asking: "was the sales change due to media, OR due to a change in search rank/inventory/pricing?" They strip out the shelf signal to isolate media lift.

DSIM's purpose is the inverse: **to quantify and surface shelf signals as actionable outputs** — to tell a brand *how much* a 7% OOS rate suppressed media ROI and what fixing it is worth.

**Additional gaps:** No evidence of content quality scoring (title, images, A+ content). No store-cluster localization. National or campaign-level only.

One-sentence differentiation: **"Incremental uses shelf data to clean the media signal. DSIM uses shelf data to surface the shelf opportunity."**

## DSIM Displacement Narrative
**Short-term complement framing:** "Incremental answers 'did your media work?' DSIM answers 'why did your media work and what shelf conditions constrained or amplified it?' Use both — they solve different questions."

**Displacement evidence:**
- "Can Incremental.com tell you how much your 78% in-stock rate was capping the incremental lift they measured? No — that's a control variable they stripped out, not a deliverable."
- "Incremental doesn't model content quality — whether your PDP was depressing organic conversion during the campaign. DSIM does."
- "Incremental measures whether your campaign was incremental. DSIM measures whether your shelf was ready for the campaign."

**Warm prospect signal:** A brand already using Incremental.com is a warm DSIM prospect — they're sold on the concept that shelf dynamics affect media outcomes. DSIM closes the loop by making shelf execution actionable.
