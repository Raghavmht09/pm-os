# Presentations & Decks

**Purpose:** Customer-facing and internal presentation materials for DSIM — QBRs, launch announcements, customer walkthroughs, strategy reviews.

## Naming Convention

- `dsim-[audience]-[purpose]-[date].md` or `.pptx`
  - Example: `dsim-customer-qbr-clorox-2026-04-20.md`
  - Example: `dsim-internal-launch-strategy-2026-04-20.md`
  - Example: `dsim-sales-battlecard-commerceiq-2026-04-20.md`

## Folder Organization (Optional Subfolders)

```
presentations/
├── customer-qbrs/          ← Quarterly business reviews per account
├── launch-decks/           ← Launch announcements and go-to-market slides
├── internal-strategy/      ← Product strategy, roadmap reviews for leadership
├── sales-enablement/       ← Battlecards, competitive positioning, discovery decks
└── workshop-materials/     ← Customer workshops, training decks
```

## What Goes Here

- Customer presentation outlines and full decks
- Sales battlecards (competitive positioning, messaging hierarchy)
- Internal leadership reviews (strategy updates, KPI reviews, roadmap decisions)
- Customer walkthroughs (feature demos, PoC results, success case studies)

## Creating Presentations

Use `/presentation-draft [topic + audience]` skill for narrative drafts.

For design and formatting, see `../../templates/` or use DataWeave brand guidelines.
